Too Much Chocolate Cake

I found this recipe several years ago on allrecipes, which is a phenomenal website with a ton of amazing recipes. This cake recipe is my all time favorite, and my number one go to when making cakes, cupcakes, or any variation of either. I've even made a vanilla version of this cake subsituting the chocolate pudding and chocolate cake for vanilla flavors. I highly recomend this recipe for any sort of get together as it usually doesn't last long and makes enough to go background for seconds. Enjoy!
